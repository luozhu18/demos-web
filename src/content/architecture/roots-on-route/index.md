Nomadic pastoralism is a sustainable and culturally significant way of life for Kazakh communities. However, settlement policies and urbanization have disrupted this tradition, particularly in education. My thesis proposes a school system that integrates formal education with the nomadic lifestyle, ensuring cultural preservation while addressing modern educational needs. The goal is to create a system that respects the Kazakh cosmology and traditions while enabling children to access quality education without abandoning their way of life. This project seeks to provide a solution that balances modernity and tradition, fostering resilience among nomadic communities in a rapidly changing world. And can such a semi-fixed strategy that exists in between towns and pastoral areas offer a framework for the development of other facilities and the future lives of nomadic peoples?
